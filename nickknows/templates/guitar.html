Nick Knows how to play Guitar


I'm a self taught guitarist that has been playing for 15 years. I was really good at the video game Guitar Hero
and I got sick of people asking me to play to show off for others, I thought it was lame and wanted to learn the
real thing. I grew up playing saxaphone, but the guitar was something I always wanted to learn so I bought my
first guitar, and Ibanex Acoustic/Electric, and went to work starting to learn. I utilized as many resources as I could,
the internet, took classes at college, and played with friends. I didn't play as much for a number of years and have
gotten back into it hard since COVID hit and I started working from home. I played so much that I bought my first new guitar
in years, a Teton dreadnaught that was my favorite sounding of all the others in the shops I went to. A new, fantastic sounding,
guitar really catapulted my playing and I played more and practiced more than ever before. My daughter loves it and I love playing music.
