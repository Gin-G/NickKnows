About Fährtbags

Why I started making one-piece snow suits

Fährtbags started because I wanted a one-piece that actually fit right and held up on the hill — and I couldn't find one I liked off the shelf. So I learned to sew and made my own.

I like making things with my hands. It's the same reason I built an arcade, restored a car, and play guitar. Sewing turned out to be one of those skills where every hour you put in shows up directly in the finished product. That kind of feedback loop is addictive.

The name is a riff on the German word for track or trail — fitting for a ski-focused brand, and a little bit absurd, which felt right.
