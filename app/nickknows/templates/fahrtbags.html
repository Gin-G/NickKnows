Fährtbags

Handcrafted one-piece snow suits — made with intention

Fährtbags is a small side project born out of a love for making things with my hands. I got into sewing and quickly found that it's one of those skills where the more you put into it, the more you get out. There's something satisfying about wearing something on the mountain that you built yourself.

The one-piece snow suit is the flagship product — fully constructed by hand, designed for function and fit on the hill. Each suit is made to order.
