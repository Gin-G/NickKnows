Understanding Snap Counts

Offensive Snaps

Number of plays where the player was on the field when their team had possession of the ball.

Defensive Snaps

Number of plays where the player was on the field when the opposing team had possession.

Special Teams Snaps

Number of special teams plays (kicks, punts, returns) where the player participated.

Snap Percentages

Percentage of available snaps the player participated in for each category.
